ERSOW.

EEPIS Robot Soccer On Wheeled

The ERSOW team is one of the robotics teams at the Politeknik Elektronika Negeri Surabaya. There is a champion slogan. Ersow also often won titles at the state and federal levels.

About Us

Our Division

Our team is divided into several divisions that have their own tasks.

Mechanic

Designing an optimal robot body and addressing all the necessary physics aspects for its functionality.

Computer Vision

Utilizing Machine Learning to detect objects around the robot through the camera.

Embedded System

Programming the microcontroller so that it can process data from the sensor accurately and swiftly.

Non Technical

In charge of managing non-technical things like Website, Instagram Post and other.

Artificial Intelligence

Programming a robot to make a Artificial Intelligence decision to complete the mission.

Communication

Create a program that connects AI and Vision along with other supporting software.

Galery

Team members build, program and play robots to win games.
